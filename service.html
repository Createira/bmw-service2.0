BMW spring check

Ice, snow and salt have taken their toll on your BMW over the winter – the battery, chassis, bodywork and glass have been subject to high demands. The BMW spring check offers you the option of focusing your BMW check on areas that have been subject to particularly high demands. So we look at the engine and gearbox as much as the braking system and lighting. This ensures that the BMW spring check gives you the confidence to know that your BMW is top-fit for the summer. And a little effort ensures that small issues do not become major repairs.

Our offer:

  • Check on liquid levels
  • Visual check on braking system, drive belts, shock absorbers
  • Check on suspension, shock absorbers, steering components
  • Check windscreen for stone chip damage
  • Check the wiper and water system
  • Wheels and tyres (condition, profile depth and tyre pressure)
  • Check on bodywork for damage

BMW holiday check

Finally off on holiday! We’ll be happy to help with the BMW holiday check so that you can be confident even when travelling far from home: Do you have the hi-vis jacket, spare bulb set and fire extinguisher on board? Is the map data in the sat nav system up-to-date? Is the tyre pressure set correctly for increased loads? Your BMW holiday check also includes numerous technical tests. And we look forward to hearing about your trip. Because the BMW holiday check also includes an external wash after the holiday.

Our offer:

  • Check on liquid levels
  • Visual check on braking system, drive belts, shock absorbers
  • Visual check on air-conditioner condenser and cooling systems for dirt
  • Check on suspension, shock absorbers and steering components
  • Light test
  • Wheels and tyres (condition, profile depth and tyre pressure)
  • External wash

BMW air conditioning check

We recommend the BMW air conditioning check in good time for the summer. Our BMW service specialists will thoroughly check whether your air conditioning system is working perfectly. They professionally clean and disinfect the cooling system in order to ensure the interior temperature is pleasant and healthy. If necessary, the coolant is exchanged. The BMW air conditioning check ensures that you can keep a cool head even on hot days and thus drive in a more relaxed way.

Our offer:

  • Check on air conditioning condenser, drive belts and micro-filter (exchange optional)
  • Air conditioner output test
  • Air conditioning system checked for build-up of odours
  • Cleaning the air conditioning system annually
  • Check on the coolant quantity
  • Cleaning the air conditioner evaporator
  • Cleaning the micro-filter casing

BMW engine oil service

A BMW engine oil service offers you more than just an oil change. It also covers a preliminary check, e.g. on brakes and tyres. It includes a comprehensive engine oil service that is matched especially to your BMW and ensures your engine will last for a long time:

  • Engine oil exchange and disposal of old oil
  • Replacement of oil filter
  • Spark plugs exchanged at every other engine oil service (petrol only)
  • Air filter insert and fuel filter exchange (diesel only) after every third BMW engine oil service
  • Micro-filter exchange

Individual search for spare parts

Finding spare parts for a car is not an easy process that requires skill and knowledge. If you understand cars at the professional level and know which part is needed for a car, use the catalogs available on the network. However, as our experience shows, such a case is rare, and most car owners still do not have enough knowledge and free time to correctly and quickly select the right spare part without harming the car.

Benefits of an individual parts search:

  • Unmistakable part identification
  • Selection of high-quality original parts
  • Saving personal time
  • additional advice on issues of interest

Welding works

Parts damaged in an accident, fractures of individual parts, corrosion and other similar problems are solved today by high-tech welding. If cracks appear on metal surfaces, it is not necessary to replace the units with new ones - the workshop specialists will weld stationary parts with high quality, so they will serve for many years. In addition to repairs, we also carry out individual external tuning work for BMW by welding.

BMW welding works at Checkpoint are:

  • Anti-corrosion treatment and sealants
  • 1 year warranty without mileage limitation
  • Compliance with the stated deadlines

Diagnostics and repair of the brake system

The slightest malfunction in the braking system endangers not only your safety, but also all road users who find themselves on the road. In order not to bring the situation to life-threatening, regular servicetenance is recommended with checking the brake system for operating efficiency.

Engine repair

As the heart of a car, the engine is one of the most complex mechanisms with a lot of electronics. It is rarely possible to blame the Bavarian manufacturer, because a failed BMW engine is probably the result of improper operation of the car and irregular servicetenance.
